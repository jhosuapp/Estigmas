Hallucinations:

These are perceptions in which the person hears, sees or feels things that originate inside his or her own brain.

Delirium:

Strongly held irrational and false beliefs produced by the inability to separate real from unreal experiences.

Epilepsy:

It is a chronic brain disease characterized by recurrent seizures that affects people worldwide..

Psychotic episode:

These are serious mental disorders that cause abnormal ideas and perceptions.

Remember that you are not alone

If you or someone close to you has symptoms or has a mental disorder, here we leave you the necessary resources. Do not be afraid to seek help!

Do you suffer from a mental disorder and do not know what to do?

Don’t worry. There are many effective treatments for those with mental disorders today, including antidepressant medications, psychotherapies, and other interventions. The important thing is that you visit a specialist to get the proper diagnosis and treatment.

Do any of your relatives suffer from a mental disorder?

A good family environment is an important factor for people with mental illness, as it makes it easier for them to have a less stressful life. The family assumes a complementary role that provides affection, support, and acceptance, making the patient feel more secure and confident in his recovery.

Professional medical advice can change your future or that of a loved one. Find here the resources that you need.

Build your emotional first-aid kit in times of pandemic

A first-aid kit has elements that allow you to attend an emergency in the best way. Here are 6 tips to help you take care of your health in times of pandemic:

Have a support group

A person who can listen to you without judging. Connecting in times of crisis is very important to alleviate discomfort.

Learn to set limits

Don't allow the wishes or expectations of others to invade your emotional space. Try to limit the amount of news you consume, the amount of work you accept and the negative ideas that invade your thoughts.

Stay in control of your emotions

In difficult moments, carry out a self-observation and identify how you feel, how affected you are and if it is necessary to ask for help.

Talk about your feelings

Although it can be difficult and even painful at times, you will be able to identify what you feel and better understand your mind. This will help cleanse your wounds and make you feel better. It favors your own growth and self-esteem.

Build your own self-care routine

In order to release the day’s tension, this will allow you to keep a good emotional condition in case of facing a crisis.

Be resilient

The capacity that human beings have that allows us to face adversity, build, grow and learn through crisis. At times like these we must consciously strengthen it.
